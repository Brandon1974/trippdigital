## Platform Strategy

### Facebook (Primary)
- **Group:** "Make Money From Home — Tripp Digital Community"
- Monitor for product ideas, sales opportunities, and engagement signals
- Free Facebook products serve as entry point to the group/list
- Post cadence: consistent, community-first content → occasional product drops

### Payhip (Revenue Hub)
- Primary storefront: payhip.com/Tinytripp
- All products link here; site embeds Payhip buy buttons
- Free products also live here (email capture)

### YouTube
- Video content strategy (transcripts go in raw/ecosystem)
- iPhone Movie Maker product suggests DIY video content production
- Higgsfield used for AI-generated video content

### Pinterest (Reactive / Trend-Driven)
- **Rule:** Any trending Pinterest topic = build PDF and list on Payhip immediately
- Pinterest is a signal source, not a posting platform
- Speed is the differentiator — spot trend → build → list same session

### Email / Newsletter
- Newsletter sold as a $10 Payhip product (Tripp Digital Insider)
- Site has newsletter signup forms (header + footer)
- Goal: build list from free lead magnets, monetize with paid newsletter + product drops

### Gumroad / Etsy / Whop
- Secondary distribution for existing products
- Gumroad: buildwithaihq.gumroad.com
- Not the primary focus — Payhip is home base

## Product Creation Triggers

1. **Pinterest trending topic** → PDF product → Payhip listing
2. **Facebook group question** repeated multiple times → PDF guide
3. **New side hustle / income niche** trending anywhere → income guide PDF
4. **Business template need** identified → template product
5. **Seasonal event** (holidays, tax season, etc.) → seasonal guide
