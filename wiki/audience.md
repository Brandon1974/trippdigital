# Audience — Tripp Digital

---

## Primary Audiences

### 1. Work-From-Home Seekers
The largest cluster of products targets people who want to earn money from home or start a side hustle. They are skeptical of scams, want practical step-by-step guides, and respond to income specificity ("$30 an hour", "this week").

**Products serving this audience:**
- $30 An Hour From Home
- 5 Ways to Make Money on Instagram for Free
- 5 Ways to Make Money on Facebook This Week
- How to Make Money on Facebook — The Complete Guide
- Work From Home as a Truck Dispatcher
- Skip the Agency: How Caregivers Get Paid Directly

**Pain points:** Don't know where to start, overwhelmed by options, afraid of upfront costs, want real proven methods.

**Language that works:** "From home", "this week", "real ways", "for free", "step by step", "no experience needed"

---

### 2. Small Business Owners & Entrepreneurs
People starting or running small businesses who need templates, tools, and guides to look professional without hiring expensive help.

**Products serving this audience:**
- Business Plan Template
- Invoice & Contract Templates Bundle
- Client Onboarding Kit
- Grant Proposal Template
- How to Start a Grant Writing Business
- How to Build a Local Web Agency

**Pain points:** Can't afford agency prices, don't know where to start, need professional-looking documents fast.

**Language that works:** "Template", "done for you", "professional", "affordable", "instant download"

---

### 3. Daycare / Childcare Providers
Small daycare operators and family childcare providers who need professional documents.

**Products serving this audience:**
- Daycare Parent Handbook Template

**Note:** This is a Pinterest-friendly niche. Opportunity to expand with more childcare business templates.

---

### 4. AI & Tech Enthusiasts
People curious about AI tools and how to use them to make money or build things.

**Products serving this audience:**
- Build With AI Complete Pack
- The AI Prompt Vault
- iPhone Movie Maker: Film Like a Pro

**Pain points:** Don't know which AI tool to start with, overwhelmed by options, want to know which prompts actually work.

---

### 5. Local Virginia Beach Businesses (Web Agency Clients)
Small businesses with no online presence — found via Google Maps prospecting.

**Service serving this audience:** Web agency ($97–$297/month)

**Pain points:** No website, losing customers to competitors who are online, don't have time to DIY.

**Language that works:** "Professional website", "get found online", "more customers", "no long contracts", "local"

## Common Buying Triggers

- Specific income claim ("$30 an hour", "make money this week")
- Free entry point (lead magnets) → paid upsell
- "Done for you" templates (low effort to value ratio)
- AI / tech curiosity
- Trending topics (Pinterest signals → immediate PDF product)
