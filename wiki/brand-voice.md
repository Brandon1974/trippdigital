# Brand Voice & Visual Identity — Tripp Digital

---

## Visual Identity

### Colors
- **Background:** Black (`#000000` or near-black `#0a0a0a`)
- **Accent:** Orange `#FF6B00`
- **Text:** White `#ffffff`

The accent color `#FF6B00` matches the existing website CSS and is the official Tripp Digital brand color. Use it on all new builds — PDFs, slideshows, spec sites, and digital products.

### Typography
- **Headings:** Bebas Neue (bold, condensed, all-caps feel)
- **Body:** Inter (clean, readable, professional)

### Logo style
- "TRIPP" in white + "DIGITAL" in orange accent
- All caps, spaced lettering

---

## Tone & Voice

### Personality
- Direct and confident (not pushy)
- Practical and results-focused
- Approachable — not corporate, not flashy
- "Real people, real solutions"

### Writing style
- Short sentences, action-oriented
- Lead with the outcome or benefit, not the feature
- Use specific numbers when possible ("$97/month", "5 pages", "10 real ways")
- All-caps for labels and section headers (matches Bebas Neue aesthetic)

### Phrases that match brand voice
- "Professional. Affordable. Local."
- "Get online in days, not months"
- "No hidden fees or long contracts"
- "Built to convert visitors"
- "Real people, real solutions"
- "Your local web partner"

### Phrases to avoid
- Hype language ("AMAZING!", "life-changing")
- Vague claims without specifics
- Corporate jargon
- Passive voice
