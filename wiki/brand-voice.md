### Logo style

---

## Tone & Voice

### Personality
- Direct and confident (not pushy)
- Practical and results-focused
- Approachable — not corporate, not flashy
- "Real people, real solutions"

### Writing style
- Short sentences, action-oriented
- Lead with the outcome or benefit, not the feature
- Use specific numbers when possible ("$97/month", "5 pages", "10 real ways")
- All-caps for labels and section headers (matches Bebas Neue aesthetic)

### Phrases that match brand voice
- "Professional. Affordable. Local."
- "Get online in days, not months"
- "No hidden fees or long contracts"
- "Built to convert visitors"
- "Real people, real solutions"
- "Your local web partner"

### Phrases to avoid
- Hype language ("AMAZING!", "life-changing")
- Vague claims without specifics
- Corporate jargon
- Passive voice
