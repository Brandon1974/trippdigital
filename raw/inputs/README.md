# raw/inputs

Stores voice notes, audio transcripts, brain dumps, meeting notes, and unfiltered ideas.

## What belongs here

- Transcribed voice notes and recordings
- Raw brain dumps (unstructured, stream-of-consciousness writing)
- Quick ideas captured on mobile or in passing
- Meeting notes before they are structured
- Product idea sketches

## Naming convention

```
YYYY-MM-DD_short-description.md
```

Examples:
- `2026-06-28_voice-note-offer-idea.md`
- `2026-07-03_brain-dump-funnel-structure.md`

## Rules

- Drop raw content here as-is. Spelling and grammar do not matter.
- Move a copy to `/process` before editing or summarizing.
- Original files stay untouched in this folder.
