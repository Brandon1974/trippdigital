# ChatGPT Prompting Guide - Narration Script (2-3 minutes)

## [SLIDE 1: Title Slide]
"Welcome to The Complete Guide to Prompting ChatGPT in 2026. I'm going to teach you the exact framework that separates mediocre prompts from exceptional ones."

## [SLIDE 2: The Core Principle]
"Here's what you need to know: Output quality is almost entirely determined by prompt quality. Vague prompts produce vague results. The gap between a mediocre prompt and a great one isn't cleverness—it's specificity."

## [SLIDE 3: Introduce PTCF]
"We're going to use the PTCF Framework. It's simple: Persona, Task, Context, Format.

This framework works for ChatGPT, and it's the foundation of great prompts."

## [SLIDE 4: Persona - Who Should ChatGPT Be?]
"Persona: Define the role or expertise ChatGPT should adopt. Are they a marketer? A developer? A strategist? Be specific about their experience level too.

Example: 'You are an experienced UX designer with 10 years of mobile app expertise.'"

## [SLIDE 5: Task - What Exactly?]
"Task: Be crystal clear about what you want ChatGPT to do. Use action verbs: Create, Write, Analyze, Compare.

Don't say 'help me with marketing.' Say 'Write a 200-word email encouraging people to sign up for our free trial.'"

## [SLIDE 6: Context - Provide Background]
"Context: Give ChatGPT all relevant information. What's your industry? Who's your audience? What constraints do you have? The more context, the better the response.

ChatGPT can't read your mind. Tell it everything it needs to know."

## [SLIDE 7: Format - How Should It Look?]
"Format: Specify exactly what you want back. Bullets? Paragraphs? A table? A code snippet? 

Be explicit. ChatGPT will follow format specifications precisely."

## [SLIDE 8: The 5-Sentence Formula]
"Here's your shortcut. Keep your prompt to 5 sentences:

One: Your role with specific expertise.
Two: The task you want done.
Three: Key details about the target or context.
Four: How to structure the response.
Five: Any important constraints or requirements.

That's it. Under 100 words, maximum impact."

## [SLIDE 9: Real Example]
"Let me show you a real example. Here's a weak prompt: 'Write something about marketing.'

Here's a strong one: 'You are a professional copywriter. Write a 150-word LinkedIn post encouraging entrepreneurs to automate their sales process. Include a statistic if you have one, use a professional but conversational tone, and end with a clear call-to-action for a free consultation.'

See the difference? The second one has everything ChatGPT needs."

## [SLIDE 10: ChatGPT's 2026 Superpowers]
"Don't forget: ChatGPT in 2026 has superpowers. It can browse the web. It can run code. It can generate images with DALL-E. It has memory across conversations.

Leverage these. Say 'Search the web for...' or 'Write and test Python code for...' Explicitly ask ChatGPT to use these tools."

## [SLIDE 11: Common Mistakes]
"Mistake one: Unclear task. Be specific, not vague.

Mistake two: No format specified. ChatGPT guesses wrong.

Mistake three: Missing context. Add background information.

Mistake four: Too many things at once. Ask one main thing per prompt.

Mistake five: Not using tools. Leverage web search, code execution, image generation."

## [SLIDE 12: Pro Tips]
"Tip one: Show examples of what you want. It's worth more than a hundred words of explanation.

Tip two: Iterate. If the first response isn't quite right, refine and ask again.

Tip three: Front-load important information. ChatGPT reads top to bottom.

Tip four: Use memory. Tell ChatGPT your preferences once, reference them forever.

Tip five: Chain prompts together. Build on previous answers for better results."

## [SLIDE 13: The Golden Rule]
"Remember this: Vague input equals vague output.

Every word you spend on clarity in your prompt saves you ten words of refinement afterward.

Invest upfront in a clear, specific, well-structured prompt. It pays off immediately."

## [SLIDE 14: Final Thought]
"That's the complete guide to prompting ChatGPT in 2026. Master the PTCF framework. Be specific. Provide context. Specify format. And leverage ChatGPT's incredible capabilities.

You'll get exceptional results every single time. Start using this framework today."
